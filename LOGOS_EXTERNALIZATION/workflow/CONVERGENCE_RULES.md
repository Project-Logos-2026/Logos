# Convergence Rules (Authoritative)

## Central Authority
All templates converge through a single arbiter.
No distributed authority exists.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Conforms to primitive list
- Slot completeness
- Deterministic phrasing
- Governance-compatible language

## Rejection Conditions
- Implicit inference
- Optional omission
- Freeform explanation
- Runtime assumptions

Rejected drafts are discarded without partial reuse.
